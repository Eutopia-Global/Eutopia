Wally Farms · Hydroponics

FarOut Container Farm

FarOut Container Farm, led by Danny Amend, grows food inside refurbished shipping containers using vertical hydroponics. The approach turns durable, movable containers into efficient farms that use a fraction of the water of conventional growing and can operate almost anywhere — including places facing water scarcity.

A 25-year culinary career, and the 2017 wildfires in his hometown of Santa Rosa, drew Danny toward climate-forward food production. At Wally Farms he's exploring how hydroponics can help feed communities in an ecologically strained world. His favorite crop to grow and eat: spicy mustard greens.
