Wally Farms · Botanical skincare

Apis Apotheca

Apis Apotheca is a farm-to-face skincare company founded by herbalist and farmer Aviva Skye Tilson. Nearly all of the herbs, flowers, and plants in its formulas are grown organically and biodynamically on the farm, then processed steps away in a solar-powered lab to preserve the vitality of freshly harvested plants.

Born from Aviva's own journey with autoimmune skin conditions, the line has helped people around the world calm acne, eczema, and psoriasis — proof that small-scale regenerative agriculture can have an outsized impact on people's lives. Everything ships in compostable packaging.
