Wally Farms · Climate

Old wisdom, new technology.

A 1,000-acre climate-action farm in the Hudson Valley, home to independent farm businesses building climate-resilient models on the land.

Pulling the future forward

Wally Farms pairs time-tested land stewardship with the tools of a low-carbon future — generating clean power on site and working toward a farm where smart machines and animals tend the land together.
