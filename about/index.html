About the Founders

Albert Wenger

Albert is an investor and co-founder of Eutopia, The Spark of Hudson, HudsonUP, HudsonDots and Wally Farms. He is also a Partner at Union Square Ventures (USV) in New York and the author of The World After Capital. He graduated with a BA from Harvard University and a PhD from M.I.T. in Information Technology. You can find his blog at continuations.com.

Gigi Danziger

Gigi is an entrepreneur, investor, and co-founder of Eutopia. She also co-founded The Spark of Hudson, HudsonUP, HudsonDots and Wally Farms and previously founded (and sold) two technology companies. A Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts, Gigi holds a BA from Cornell University and JD from New York University School of Law.
