Our philosophy

An optimistic vision + the attention to build it

We are building Eutopia, which means a "good place." Eutopia comes from the Greek "eu" (good) and "topos" (place). That's unlike Utopia, which means "nowhere." Our good place isn't a fantasy — it's something we build, one initiative at a time, making our communities, our regions, and ultimately all of Earth a good place for humans and other species alike.

Our values

  • Optimism

    Problems are solvable, and a better world is achievable, so we approach them with conviction and hope.

  • Fun

    As we build a world in which we can all thrive, we want to have lots of fun along the way. We call it "serious fun."

  • Attention

    Attention is the foundation of all personal and collective agency, so we commit to the lifelong cultivation of our capacity for presence.

  • Community

    Interpersonal connection is the cornerstone of a fulfilling life, so we build strong local communities rooted in our shared humanity, celebrating our individual diversity.

  • Trust

    Progress depends on cooperation, so we lead with trust — keeping our commitments, assuming good faith, and supporting one another across our differences.

  • Curiosity

    Understanding how the world works is how we improve it, so we stay curious — approaching ideas with humility, creativity, and openness to criticism.

We work in concentric circles: caring first for ourselves, then those closest to us, then our community, then the world -- sometimes in sequence, sometimes all at once.
