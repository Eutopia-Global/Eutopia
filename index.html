Our philosophy

An optimistic vision — and the grit to build it.

Humanity has an amazing new set of capabilities, from artificial intelligence to clean energy to synthetic biology. The question of our moment is what we should be working toward given our extraordinary powers. Eutopia comes from the Greek "Eu" (good) and "topos" (place) — unlike Utopia, which means "nowhere," Eutopia is about making our communities, our cities, our regions, and ultimately all of Earth a good place for humans and other species. The best initiatives come from people genuinely taking care of one another. We learn from each other and from the world at large — and then we get to work building models that make a better future real, one initiative at a time.

  • Optimism

    Problems are solvable and a better world is achievable, so we approach them with joyful optimism.

  • Attention

    Attention is the foundation of all personal and collective agency, so we commit to the lifelong cultivation of our capacity for presence.

  • Community

    Interpersonal connection is the cornerstone of a fulfilling life, so we build strong local communities rooted in our shared humanity and celebrating our individual diversity.

  • Love

    Humanity's power to both create and destroy grows exponentially, so we operate from a foundation of universal love and embrace our responsibility to promote the flourishing of all conscious life.

  • Truth

    Knowledge, humility, and understanding are the keys to solving any problem, so we approach ideas with curiosity, creativity, and openness to criticism.

We work in concentric circles — care for yourself first, then those closest to you, then your community, then the world at large. Sometimes sequentially, others concurrently.
